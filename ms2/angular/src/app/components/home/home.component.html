Welcome to our Cinema!

Our cinema offers two types of tickets:

  1. Regular tickets
  2. Streaming tickets

Regular tickets offer the traditional move watching experience. The Streaming tickets were specifically designed to tackle the COVID-19 challenges and to provide movie lovers the next best possible solution for watching the latest movies. Using the streaming ticket is very simple and straightforward, and can be done in a matter of few steps:

  • Purchase the streaming ticket
  • Check under My Account -> My Tickets

New! - 4DX enabled movies

We are proud to announce the we are among the first cinemas to offer the new 4DX technology. The 4DX technology can only be experience on-site. 4DX offers an even more immersive and realistic experience while watching movies.
